About

I'm a full stack developer with a warm personality, a boatload of skills, and a desire to make things that matter.

Throughout my career, I've worn many hats including: researcher, designer, full stack developer, product owner, strategic advisor to executives, and leader of high performing teams. No matter the role, I have always used technology to connect people, improve processes, and share knowledge. Now, I also build it.

I use my creative, technology, and strategy talents to build meaningful tools that work well and look great. I'm known for producing empathy driven user experiences, meticulously planning and tracking my projects, developing crisp, responsive solutions, having an eagle eye for spotting and resolving bugs, and bringing a little bit of play to everything I do.

When I'm not tweeting about bad UX or organizing my Trello boards, you can find me hanging with my husband, Mike, and our dog, Juneau, planning a trip to an international destination or National Park, watching Netflix and HBO, or listening to my latest downloads on PocketCast and Audible.
